Hiccup Stopper: a helper invention for stopping users hiccupping when they are alone


The Hiccup Stopper helps users to stop hicups through pouring water when it detects hiccup and making loud noises randomly to scare them.

Motivation


Many of us have the experience of hiccupping non-stop. It is not a serious problem but quit annoying. But it is something that we can all relate to. By creating this Hiccup Stopper, it provides people with an interesting solution. My design concept is to create a machine that helps people stop their hiccup when they are alone. In China, there is a special chinese prescription that scaring someone will help him stop his hiccup. On the other hand, a universal stop-hiccup method is to drink as much as water possible. According to the clinical references, it can be proved these two methods are valid: ‚ÄúDrinking a glass of water quickly‚Äù and ‚ÄúHaving someone frighten you‚Äù can help stop hicupping [1].

Therefore I integrate these two methods into my design to create an interactive installation, called ‚ÄúHiccup Stopper‚Äù.

System Features

My prototype is a square looking device with a tube sticking out. When the user cannot keep hiccupping, He can turn on the machine and put a cup under the tube.


Water pouring


it will pour water when the noise is made, and it may detect other voices aside from hiccups. Therefore it may pour water when there is no cup under, which will cause a hilarious disaster on the desk.


‚ÄúScare‚Äù Mechanism


When the machine detects hiccup, it will pour him a small amount of water to remind him to take a sip of water. Besides, it will make a sudden and loud noise with vibration once a while to scare the user, in order to help him stop hiccupping.

Implementation


I use a mini mp3 player to create the loud noise. The in and out of the water is controlled by a plunger connected to a motor. And the sound detect is made true by a sound detector. The vibration is realized by a vibration motor. When the analog input of the sound detector pass a certain value, the motor will be triggered and it will plunge the plunger out for two seconds and then close it up again. The loud sound and the vibration motor will be activated every 60 seconds.

Reflection


Although this intended purpose is achieved, this device still have some limitations that can be improved. First the hicccup is soley detected by the level of sound, other noises may also trigger it. Secondly, the jump scare voices is limited, so once the user is used to this voice, the effectiveness may be weaken. Another proplem is that it can not detect whether there is a cup below the water outlet, which may cause some problems.

Overall, the hiccup stopper is a novel and fun inevntion to help people stop hiccupping when they are alone.Although it may not work as expected and causes disaster on the desk, it is still an interesting invention that people can echo with and make them spot this interesting experience in life. Therefore, it matches the definition of useless invention.
